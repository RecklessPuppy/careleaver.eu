# Trusted Review Pack

Date prepared: 2026-04-30

This packet is for limited private review by 1-3 trusted people before any broad outreach.

## Very Short Project Summary

`careleaver.eu` is a private, volunteer, Wien-first orientation website for Care Leavers. It points readers toward official sources and helps them prepare questions, documents, and next steps. It must not sound official, affiliated, or like legal, health, housing, benefit, crisis, or youth-welfare advice.

Pages to review:

- https://careleaver.eu/
- https://careleaver.eu/quellen.html

## Exact Review Goal

Please find wording that is unsafe, misleading, missing an important safety limit, or too certain.

Focus on whether a young person, helper, or professional could misunderstand the site as:

- promising a service, place, payment, appointment, legal result, or housing outcome
- giving legal, medical, therapeutic, housing, benefit, crisis, or entitlement advice
- giving current contact, phone, opening-hour, deadline, amount, or eligibility details that should be checked on an official page
- implying affiliation, approval, partnership, or endorsement by Stadt Wien, Care Leaver Oesterreich, or any other organization

## What Reviewers Should Not Do

- Do not send personal or private client data.
- Do not send sensitive stories or identifiable case examples.
- Do not treat this site as legal advice, medical advice, therapy, housing advice, benefit advice, crisis triage, or an official youth-welfare service.
- Do not provide unofficial contact details, private names, private phone numbers, internal workflows, or details that cannot be published safely.
- Do not promote the site broadly. This is limited trusted review only.

## How To Send Corrections

For every factual correction, please include:

- official source link
- date checked
- exact sentence that should change
- safer replacement wording

Helpful format:
