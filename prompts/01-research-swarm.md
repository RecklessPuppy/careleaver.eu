# Prompt 01: Research Swarm

Use this prompt for source research and claim auditing.

```text
We are working on careleaver.eu, a Wien-first Austrian Care Leaver resource site.

Please run a research pass on the current public content.

Tasks:
1. Read AGENTS.md, SOURCE_POLICY.md, CONTENT_SAFETY.md, STATE.md, and index.html.
2. Identify all source-sensitive claims in index.html:
   - legal rights
   - benefits and money
   - emergency/crisis/health
   - housing
   - child/youth welfare and aftercare
   - contacts, phone numbers, addresses, opening hours, forms, and deadlines
3. Create or update research/source-log.md with official sources where available.
4. Put missing, weak, stale, or unclear claims into research/open-questions.md.
5. Summarize patterns and recommendations in research/research-synthesis.md.

Do not redesign the site.
Do not make public factual claims stronger unless the source log supports them.
Use official Austria/Wien sources first.
```
